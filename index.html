Health Analytics FAQ

The Body Mass Index (BMI) is a universal screening tool for health risks. Our calculator follows the latest 2026 WHO clinical guidelines.

Is BMI accurate for gym-goers? BMI measures total mass. If you have high muscle density, consult a specialist for a body fat percentage analysis.

What is the 'Healthy' range? A BMI between 18.5 and 24.9 is considered optimal for minimizing metabolic health risks.

How often should I check my BMI? For active weight management, a monthly check is recommended to track trends in your Health Intelligence Engine.
